The laws that govern the functioning of the Gujarat Police are primarily based on a combination of state and central laws, as Gujarat is a state in India. Here are some key laws and regulations that apply to the Gujarat Police:


1.The Gujarat Police Act, 1951:
This is the primary legislation that outlines the organization, powers, functions, and duties of the police in the state of Gujarat. It provides the legal framework for the functioning of the police department in the state.
2.The Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (CrPC):
The CrPC is a central law that governs the procedures for the investigation and trial of criminal cases in India. It applies to the Gujarat Police, as well as to police departments across the country.
3.The Indian Penal Code (IPC):
The IPC is another central law that defines various criminal offenses and their penalties. The Gujarat Police use the IPC to register and investigate cases in the state.
4.The Gujarat Police Manual:
While not a law, the Gujarat Police Manual is a set of guidelines and procedures that the police in the state follow. It provides detailed instructions on various aspects of policing, including department organization, police procedures, and handling different types of cases.
5.The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015:
This law deals with the rights and protection of children in conflict with the law. It outlines the procedures to be followed when dealing with juveniles involved in criminal activities.
6.The Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989:
This act provides special provisions for the protection of the rights of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes and the prevention of atrocities against them.
7.The Right to Information Act, 2005:
This law allows citizens to access information from public authorities, including the police, to promote transparency and accountability.
8.The Criminal Laws (Gujarat Amendment) Act, 2015:
This act introduced amendments to certain criminal laws in Gujarat, affecting issues like bail, investigation procedures, and penalties for certain offenses.

Please note that the specifics of police laws and regulations in Gujarat may change over time, and it's crucial to consult the most up-to-date and official sources or legal professionals for the latest information. The Gujarat State Police Department is a valuable resource for accessing the latest laws and regulations that pertain to the Gujarat Police.
